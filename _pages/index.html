{{{ iconmonstr 'about' }}}About

Async is an inclusive web tech meetup based in Brighton, UK. We meet on the first Thursday of the month to learn, discuss and play with web technologies. Everyone is welcome, from experienced professionals to those learning to code.

We host talks, hack nights, show n' tells and community events, all of which are free. Doors open 7.00pm, main session begins 7.30pm, we end around 9.00pm and continue nearby for an evening social. Why not join us to learn about the web!
